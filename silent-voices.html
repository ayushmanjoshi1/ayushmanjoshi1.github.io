Unspoken words...

There’s a weight in my chest I cannot name —

emotions fluent in my heart but foreign to my tongue.

Sometimes I understand myself best in silence,

because when I try to speak, the words fall apart.

It’s a storm without thunder, a scream stitched into quiet,

and I stand stranded between knowing and understanding myself,

lost not in pain, but in the raw, real, and unnamed feeling.
